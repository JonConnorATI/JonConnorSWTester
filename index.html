About me

I am a software tester and believe the role of a tester is much more than finding bugs. My view is that the tester is an advocate for the end users and should be involved in the Development Lifecycle from the beginning.

For me being part of a team that provides software solutions to its clients and hearing the words, "This is exactly what we were looking for" is what its all about.

Behavior Driven Development (BDD)

There are millions of articles, books, webpages that are devoted to the topic, so I won’t parrot what you may already know or tell you what you can easily find out yourself.

For me, the most powerful concept of BDD is the collaboration between developers and non-technical users so that the actual end users are involved in the development from day one.

The result, software is delivered faster.
